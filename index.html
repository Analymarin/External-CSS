Basic Crepes Recipe

Here is a simple but delicious crepe batter which can be made in minutes. It's made from ingredients that everyone has on hand

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of All-purpose Flour
  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 tablespoons of butter, melted


Directions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour and the eggs. Gradually add in the milk and water,
    stirring to combine. Add the salt and butter; beat until smooth.
  2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle,
    using approximately 1/4 cup for each crepe. Tilt the pan with a circular motion so that the batter coats the surface evenly.
  3. Cook the crepe for about 2 minutes, until the bottom is light brown. Loosen with a spatula, turn and cook the other side. Serve hot.
